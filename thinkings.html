Multimodal car VUI

published on Jan 10, 2021

Current VUIs, especially car VUIs are still not perfect. When a chatbot fails to offer a satisfying answer, people are sometimes annoyed and it lowers user experience.
This design seeks to help voice assistants understand users' mood and aid voice assistants in cars with facial expression understanding, so that AI can change its VUI strategy accordingly, for example, interrupting itself if an unsatisfying answer is provided. AI can also use this extra layer of information to improve itself. Since people in the car don't move frequently, a fixed camera can easily capture people's facial expressions, which is hard to achieve in other scenarios.

Slide for Food

published on Jan 10, 2021

When ordering takeouts, are you risk tolerant or risk averse? This design helps you select food via between risk tolerant and risk averse. For instance, if you always order spicy food, options in risk averse may be lots of spicy food, while in risk tolerant you may see lots of food you haven't tried before (you may like it, you may not). Slide to adjust the proportion of 'risk' you want to bear.
